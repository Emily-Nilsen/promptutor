### SWOT analysis and research

Conduct a thorough SWOT analysis for a business idea, incorporating market size research and examining the competitor landscape. Explore potential risks, identify opportunities, and provide a well-rounded evaluation for informed decision-making and successful implementation.

{% callout type="warning" title="Prompt formula" %}
Assess the feasibility of [business idea] through a comprehensive SWOT analysis, incorporating a thorough examination of the [market size] and conducting in-depth research of the [competitor landscape]. Additionally, offer valuable insights on potential [risk factors] that could impact the venture and identify key [opportunities] that may drive success. Provide a well-rounded evaluation to empower informed decision-making and increase the likelihood of successfully implementing the business idea.
{% /callout %}

{% callout title="Example 1" %}
Assess the feasibility of a sustainable online grocery delivery service through a comprehensive SWOT analysis, incorporating a thorough examination of the urban market size in major metropolitan areas and conducting in-depth research of the existing competitor landscape, including established grocery chains and emerging delivery startups. Additionally, offer valuable insights on potential logistic challenges and supply chain disruptions that could impact the venture and identify key opportunities, such as partnerships with local organic farms and targeting eco-conscious consumers, that may drive success. Provide a well-rounded evaluation to empower informed decision-making and increase the likelihood of successfully implementing the business idea.
{% /callout %}

{% callout title="Example 2" %}
Assess the feasibility of a subscription-based virtual fitness platform through a comprehensive SWOT analysis, incorporating a thorough examination of the market size for online fitness services and conducting in-depth research of the competitor landscape, including established fitness apps and virtual workout platforms. Additionally, offer valuable insights on potential challenges related to user retention and online user experience that could impact the venture and identify key opportunities, such as providing live interactive classes and partnering with fitness influencers for marketing, that may drive success. Provide a well-rounded evaluation to empower informed decision-making and increase the likelihood of successfully implementing the business idea.
{% /callout %}

{% callout title="Example 3" %}
Assess the feasibility of an AI-driven personalized e-learning platform through a comprehensive SWOT analysis, incorporating a thorough examination of the education technology market size and conducting in-depth research of the competitor landscape, including established online learning platforms and AI-powered educational tools. Additionally, offer valuable insights on potential scalability issues and user acceptance barriers that could impact the venture and identify key opportunities, such as partnering with schools for curriculum integration and targeting niche subjects with high demand for personalized learning, that may drive success. Provide a well-rounded evaluation to empower informed decision-making and increase the likelihood of successfully implementing the business idea.
{% /callout %}
